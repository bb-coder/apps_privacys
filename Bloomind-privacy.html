Bloomind Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Summary: Bloomind is a fully offline application. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data. Period.

1. Overview

Bloomind ("the App") is developed to help you record and organize your thoughts using on-device artificial intelligence. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data Collection

We collect NO data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reports sent to external services
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No cookies or tracking technologies
  • No account registration required

3. Network Usage

Bloomind makes zero network requests. The App operates entirely offline. There are no servers, no APIs, no cloud services, and no internet connectivity required or used.

4. On-Device Processing

All AI processing occurs exclusively on your device using locally stored machine learning models. Your voice recordings, text notes, photos, and generated "Brain Flowers" never leave your device.

5. Device Permissions

The App requests the following permissions, all of which are used solely for local functionality:

  • Microphone: To record voice memos ("Murmurs"). Audio is processed and stored locally only.
  • Camera: To capture photos for inclusion in your notes. Photos are stored locally only.
  • Photo Library: To import existing photos into your notes. Selected photos are copied locally.
  • Speech Recognition: To transcribe voice recordings into searchable text. Processing occurs on-device.

6. Data Storage

All your data (murmurs, brain flowers, settings) is stored exclusively in your device's local storage using Apple's CoreData framework. Data is protected by your device's built-in security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).

7. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not have access to any of your data. There are no third-party SDKs, analytics services, or advertising networks integrated into the App.

8. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data from any user, we inherently comply with COPPA and other children's privacy regulations.

9. Data Deletion

You can delete all App data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual murmurs or brain flowers within the App
  • Uninstalling the App (removes all associated data)

10. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update it within the App and on this page. However, our core commitment to zero data collection will not change.
